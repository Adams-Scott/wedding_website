FAQ

✦
What is the RSVP deadline? ▾
Please RSVP by July 1st, 2026. We kindly ask that you let us know as soon as possible so we can finalize our headcount with the venue and caterers.
What time should I arrive? ▾
The ceremony begins at 5:00 PM sharp. We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early to find your seat and settle in. Doors will open at 4:30 PM.
Where should I park? ▾
Complimentary parking is available on-site at Grey Gables Estate. Attendants will guide you to the designated parking area upon arrival. We also recommend rideshare options if you plan to enjoy cocktails.
Where should I stay? ▾
We have reserved a room block at a nearby hotel. More details, including a booking code, will be shared with guests who RSVP. There are also several wonderful boutique hotels and vacation rentals in the area.
Will there be transportation? ▾
We will be providing a shuttle between the hotel room block and the venue. Shuttle schedule details will be shared closer to the wedding date. Rideshare services are also readily available in the area.
What should I wear? ▾
The dress code is cocktail attire. Think garden party elegance — flowy dresses, linen suits, and pastel tones are always welcome. Please keep in mind the ceremony is outdoors, so comfortable footwear is a must. We love a heel but a wedge or flat is always a great idea on grass!
